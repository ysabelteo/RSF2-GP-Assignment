=== USER MANUAL ===

=== Hand ===
Press 'W' to move up hand
Press 'S' to move down hand
Press 'H' to switch jack
Press 'J' to switch back hand
Press 'C' to use power

=== Leg ===
Press 'Z' to Walk
Press '8' to jump

=== Camera ===
Press '0' to activate rotation of camera
Press 'Up' key to turn up the camera
Press 'Down' key to turn down of the camera
Press 'Left' key to turn left of the camera
Press 'Right' key to turn right of the camara

Press '9' to translate the camera
if '9' key is activated
Press 'Up' key to zoom in
Press 'Down' key to zoom out
Press 'Left' key to move camera left
Press 'Right' key to move camera right

Press 'O' to activate Ortho projection
Press 'P' to activate perspective projection

=== Lighting ===
Press 'L' to turn on/off the light.
